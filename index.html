Model Assumptions & Scope

  • Framework: Black–Scholes–Merton (BSM), European options, no early exercise.
  • Dynamics: Underlying follows GBM with constant drift and volatility (Base).
  • Rates/Dividends: Constant risk-free rate r and continuous dividend yield q.
  • Implied Vol: Base uses constant σ; Advanced supports SVI surface (market flavor).
  • Greeks: All Greeks are analytical under BSM; shown per year units when applicable.
  • Numerics: Stability clamps: T ≥ 0.02, σ ≥ 0.05, |d₁|,|d₂| ≤ 8; WebGL surfaces with LOD.
  • Axes: k = ln(K/F(T)) where F(T) = S·e^{(r−q)T}; ATM at k=0.
  • Scope: Educational visualization; not investment advice; no guarantees of market completeness.
  • Limits: No American exercise, jumps, stochastic rates/vol, or transaction costs.
